Gana Sai Teja Velicheti

Researcher  ·  Computer Vision & Machine Learning

I work on problems at the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and low-resource Indic script recognition. My current focus is on document understanding and glyph representation learning for scripts like Telugu, building robust OCR pipelines that do not need massive labeled datasets. I am also interested in how vision-language models can be adapted to languages and scripts that are severely underrepresented in pretraining data.
